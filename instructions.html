Square Off

OBJECTIVE: Square Off is played with 2-4 players. Take turns selecting tiles on the grid to form squares and diamonds to score points. The player with the most points once all the tiles are filled wins!

Squares

Form a Square by selecting the tiles that make up the 4 corners. You do not have to fill in the entire perimeter.

Diamonds

Form a Diamond by selecting the top, right, bottom, and left tiles that are equidistant from a center tile. You do not have to fill in the entire perimeter.

Rectangles

Rectangles will not earn you any points. Only Squares and Diamonds.

Scoring

The point value of a Square is determined by its area.

The outer 8x8 blue square is worth 64 points

The outer 6x6 red square is worth 36 points

The inner 4x4 blue square is worth 16 points

The inner 2x2 red square is worth 4 points

The point value of a Diamond is worth 2X its area.

The outer 4x4 blue diamond is worth 32 points

The outer 3x3 red diamond is worth 18 points

The inner 2x2 blue diamond is worth 8 points

Multipliers

You can increase the amount of points your squares and diamonds are worth by forming multiple shapes with a single move. The number of shapes you form in a single move is the multiplier that is applied to your score for that turn.

(4x4 Square + 4x4 Square) X 2 = 64 points

(3x3 Diamond + 3x3 Square) X 2 = 54 points

In order to retain and continue to increase your multiplier, you must form multiple shapes in consecutive turns, as shown by the blue player.

If you form 1 shape or fewer after a turn in which you formed multiple shapes to earn a multiplier, you will lose your multiplier, as shown by the red player.
